Job Description - Student Coordinator

Role Description

He/She will be the member of our training team.

Work Experience

  • Any Graduate/Post Graduate/Diploma holder with Computer Science/Information Technology background like BCA/MCA/B.Sc/BTech/MTech.
  • Experienced ones/freshers can apply. Candidates with teaching experience/Software Industrial experience is preferred.
  • Skill set the candidates should possess.
  • 1. Working knowledge of G-Drive, G-Sheets & G-Docs.
  • 2. Good communication skills.
  • 3. Good leadership & team management skills.
  • 4. Exposure to software technologies is an added advantage.

Job requirements

  • Each student coordinator will be having atleast 40-50 Brocamp Attendees from different batches to manage.
  • Certain batches will be assigned & advisors are requested to keep track of students from workshop to placement. Supervises those batch and provides guidance and instructions for the purpose of achieving a group of aligned results.
  • Schedule and coordinate reviews with the technical team.
  • Assesses student skills, aptitudes, abilities in order to assist in academics & career planning.
  • Advice students in developing a workplace culture, diligence and taking responsibilities.
  • Keep a track record of attendees progress level.
  • Manage attendance of Brocamp attendees
  • Review weekly tasks of Brocamp attendees.
  • Provide personal and professional advices as appropriate.
  • Implements & enforces code of conduct. Make decisions regarding these regulations as delegated.
  • Orient & give guidelines to new attendees when they join Brocamp.
  • Create and share the folders related to tasks in google drive.
  • Coordinate Termination, Quit, Batch shift, Re-Interview procedures of attendees.
  • Assign weekly tasks to each batch attendees.
  • Plan & update new weekly tasks with the help of the technical team.
  • Clear the doubts related to the task.
  • Arrange & conduct the fumigation interviews.
  • Arrange & conduct badge assigning interviews & give badges to Attendees.
  • Approve the leave of attendees
