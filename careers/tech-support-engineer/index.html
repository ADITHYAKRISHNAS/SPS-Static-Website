Job Description - Tech Support Engineer

We are looking for a Technical Support Engineer to provide assistance to our customers.

Job Type: Full Time

Responsibilities

  • You will be responsible for diagnosing and troubleshooting basic programming problems.
  • Technical Support Engineer responsibilities include using remote desktop connections to provide immediate support.
  • You will use chat applications to give clients quick answers to basic programming issues.
  • Gather and analyse information about the user’s issue and determine the best way to resolve their problem

Qualifications

  • Good understanding of computer systems, mobile devices and other tech products
  • Ability to diagnose and troubleshoot basic programming
  • Familiarity with remote desktop applications and help desk software
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
  • Ability to provide step-by-step technical help, both written and verbal.
  • Hands-on experience with Windows/Linux/Mac OS environments.
  • Ultimately, you will be a person our customer’s trust. They will rely on you to provide timely and accurate solutions to their technical problems

Experience

  • Technical support: 0 - 1 years
