SPS in Brief?

School of Practical Studies (SPS) helps the young Indian generation who had benefited nothing from our country’s poor education system by providing an intensive industry relevant training on the leading software technologies. The SPS program is completely FREE upfront and our students pay zero tuition fee until they land a job.

We believe everyone should get the right opportunities to learn, hence we do not charge any fees upfront. We work as a team because we get paid only when you get paid.

SPS is not just another training institution when you go learn something and leave forever; SPS is a community of high performance people who support each other and to win together. Each SPS grad will always be in touch with our community and help the new buddies as a way of giving back.

What is an ISA and how does it work?

Shabeer Feb 15, 2:30 AM

An ISA or Income Share Agreement is used for paying SPS tuition once you have got a job at the salary you deserve. You will pay 15% of your monthly income for a maximum period of 36 months or a maximum cap amount of Rs 5 lakhs. Since SPS does not charge any fees upfront, the ISA model incentivises us to provide superior training and access to the highest paying opportunities for you.
